These roles taught me adaptability, creativity, and the art of transforming complex ideas into clear, engaging experiences.

Throughout my travels, I continued creating art and producing music, maintaining a constant thread of creative expression. When I discovered coding, it felt like a natural progression – a way to blend my technical background, artistic sensibility, and problem-solving skills into front-end development.
