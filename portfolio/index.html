About Me

I am a Front End Web Developer with a passion for creating beautiful and functional websites. I have experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS. I am always looking to learn new technologies and improve my skills.

I am also available for freelance work. If you would like to hire me to build a website for you, please contact me using the form below.
